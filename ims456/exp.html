Step into the captivating world of Saidatul Nor Atiqah's diverse and thrilling experiences! From 2018 to 2022, I took the plunge into entrepreneurship by becoming an agent of the renowned Mamasab Bakery, a beloved and famous bakery shop in Malaysia. Specializing in a delightful array of delectable cakes and desserts, Mamasab Bakery offered its scrumptious treats at affordable prices, attracting a vast and loyal customer base. I showcased my exceptional business acumen and dedication, driving the bakery's success to new heights and achieving an impressive four-digit income during that period. But the entrepreneurial journey didn't stop there! In 2022, driven by a burning desire to explore new horizons and as part of my bachelor's degree requirements, I took on the exciting role of agent for Freshly Baked by Maiya. This decision was spurred by the bakery's immense popularity on TikTok, where it had garnered quite a reputation for its tantalizing array of dip cookies, available in a myriad of mouthwatering flavors. I was quick to recognize the potential of this trend and skillfully harnessed the allure of crunchy, delicious cookies to captivate customers, resulting in an impressive three-digit income that continues to flourish to this day. As if the bakery ventures weren't enough to satiate their thirst for success, I am embarking on another thrilling journey in 2023. Armed with a passion for design and creativity, I ventured into the world of freelance graphic design services. This bold step was in fulfillment of a bachelor's subject requirement, wherein I had to take on 50 freelance jobs. With an unwavering commitment to excellence and a flair for artistic expression, I have not only met but exceeded this challenging task, delivering impeccable designs and earning the accolades of satisfied clients. The fascinating tapestry of my experiences is woven with ambition, resilience, and a keen eye for opportunities. From my remarkable achievements as a successful agent for two iconic bakeries to my foray into the world of freelance graphic design, I with enthusiasm, emerging victorious each time. As visitors to Saidatul Nor Atiqah's website, you are invited to witness this thrilling journey of entrepreneurship and creativity. It's a tale that showcases the power of passion, hard work, and seizing opportunities. Whether you're a budding entrepreneur seeking inspiration, a fellow graphic designer in search of a role model, or simply a curious soul eager to explore captivating success stories, my experiences will leave you inspired and motivated.
