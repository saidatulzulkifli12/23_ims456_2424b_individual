My Hobby

Welcome to the hobby journey of Saidatul Nor Atiqah! The world of electrifying beats, mesmerizing performances, and enchanting melodies comes to life. I am thrilled to share with you my ultimate passion and guilty pleasure, diving headfirst into the captivating universe of Korean pop songs and indulging in the mesmerizing performances by none other than the phenomenal group, EXO! A world where music transcends language barriers, and emotions are conveyed through electrifying dance moves and soulful harmonies. That's precisely what EXO has done for me, and I am absolutely giddy to express my profound adoration for this extraordinary group. At the heart of my musical journey lies a quartet of melodies that has resonated with my soul like no other. Brace yourselves as I take you on a sonic adventure through my personal favorites, which "Love Me Right," "Kokobop," "Love Shot," and the latest one, "Cream Soda." Each of these tracks possesses a unique charm that has me hooked from the first beat. But let me tell you a little secret. I adore every single one of EXO's songs. From their debut to the latest chart-toppers, each melody brings something unique to the table, leaving me in awe of their sheer talent and versatility. From high-energy anthems to heartfelt ballads, they have got it all. If you are looking to discover a musical journey that transcends borders and sparks an insatiable passion, then join me in the enchanted realm of EXO's music. Whether you are a seasoned K-pop aficionado or just dipping your toes into this captivating world, you are bound to be captivated by the sheer magic they bring to the stage. Until then, keep the music alive and our hearts brimming with joy!
